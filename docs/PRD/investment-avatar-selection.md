### Description

Allow users to select from at least three distinct investment advisor personas (The Value Vanguard, The Innovation Instigator, The Main Street Maverick), each with a unique investment philosophy. The selected avatar will influence the tone, recommendations, and approach of all interactions.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Users can view a gallery of available investment avatars with descriptions of their philosophies
2. Each avatar has a unique visual identity (name, avatar image, color scheme)
3. Users can select their preferred avatar during onboarding or change it later in settings
4. The selected avatar's philosophy influences all recommendations and responses
5. Users can switch avatars at any time without losing their portfolio or history
